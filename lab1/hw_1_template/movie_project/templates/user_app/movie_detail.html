"Monkey Man" is an action thriller directed by and starring Dev Patel, making his directorial debut. The film centers around an anonymous young man, known as Kid, who embarks on a quest for vengeance
against corrupt leaders responsible for his mother's death and the systemic victimization of the poor and powerless. The story intertwines elements of Indian mythology, particularly the legend of Hanuman, with social commentary
on corruption, discrimination, and the plight of marginalized communities, including the Hijra community in India. The film features intense, brutal action sequences and a deeply emotional narrative, exploring themes of trauma, redemption,
and justice. Kid, after enduring years of hardship, trains rigorously and ultimately confronts the corrupt figures, including a fake guru named Baba Shakti and a ruthless police chief, Rana Singh, who were responsible for his village's destruction.
